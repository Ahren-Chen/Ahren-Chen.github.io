Summary

I volunteered at 360 Kids Youthrive from January to June 2018 as a student assistant. My role in the programs was to help the staff set up any activities that were planned for the day, interact with children, and promote a fun healthy environment for the children to thrive in. I usually attended after school, and it was an incredible feeling to know that my efforts made other people's days just a little better than before.

What I have Learned

I volunteered the year before COVID made the program go online, so I got to experience the entire process of planning and spending time with children in person. I got to see how much thought was put into each activity and how the amazing staff was considering each child individually to make sure no one got left out. I helped the staff encourage the children to talk and ensure they felt comfortable at all times while at the program, and it was rewarding to know that they appreciated it.

This volunteering experience showed me what it means to make a difference in others' lives and how fulfilling that feeling is, which I feel is invaluable going forwards.
